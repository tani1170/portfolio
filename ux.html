ux

Today UX is an integral part of the development of digital user interfaces and the discipline’s work area, tools and methods spread across the five subject areas of the education. This tonic provided a basis understanding of the interaction between users and user interfaces, as well as the theories, tools and methods you can use for research, design and testing in a digital product development.

Understanding this interaction is important as design choices and product development are based on concrete insights about real users instead of diffuse gut feelings and immediate assumptions.
