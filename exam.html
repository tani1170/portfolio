exam

During this topic a study portfolio was created which presents the projects that have been made during the first semester. The portfolio website was also a product for the exam, and its content, code and design were included in the subsequent oral exam.

exams product

The study portfolio is inspired by own daily enjoyment found in natural surroundings. The inspiration for the design of the portfolio comes from organic material, primarily plants and material found on the surface of sand on the beach.
Styletile and documentation of the creation of this website are shown below.

The websites navigation is divided intro two sections, where the first section show the work from the topics of the first semester and the second section contains information about work experience and education as well as contact information. Halfway through the assignment, the color scheme of the website was adjusted. All elements appearing in the background of each page are created in illustrator, using pen tools to create the shapes.
